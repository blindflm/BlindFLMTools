Blind FLM Heuristic Rules

The placement of the mental act operator in blind FLM is governed by a series of heauristic rules that are extended form the original KLM. These rules are as follows:

  • Rule 0: insert M operators in front of all F, T, DT, and D operators.
  • Rule 1: remove M operators that appear between two operators anticipated to appear next to each other.
  • Rule 2: remove M operators that are related to one cognitive unit expect the first M.
  • Rule 3: remove M operators that precede consecutive terminators.
  • Rule 4: remove M operators that preceded terminators of commands.
